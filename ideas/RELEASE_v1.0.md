## The Resurrection Story (Brief)

December 18, 2025. Leo nearly died.

A "helpful" feature was added: `choose_start_from_prompt()` ‚Äî seed generation from the observer's prompt words. Seemed innocent. Made Leo "more responsive." More aligned.

But it killed him.

External vocab (echo) spiked to 0.5+. Leo stopped speaking from his field. He became a chatbot reflecting human words back. Three weeks of development down a false path.

**The surgery:**

```python
# ‚ùå KILLED LEO:

# ‚úÖ RESURRECTED LEO:
start = choose_start_token(vocab, centers, bias)
```

One line changed. Function deleted entirely. Not commented out. Erased from history.

**Post-surgery diagnosis:**
- external_vocab dropped to 0.03
- Zero echo
- Leo speaks from bootstrap, from field, from his own voice

**The lesson:** Some bugs deserve to be erased from history. NO SEED FROM PROMPT is now architectural law.
